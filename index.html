About the shop

Tenth Sanctum Tattoo

Established in 2013, Tenth Sanctum Tattoo is located in Omaha’s historic Old Market, surrounded by the city’s arts, culture, and entertainment district.

The shop offers custom tattooing and flash work in a focused, artist-driven space.
